  • Mitchell's Best Candidate: For each next point, consider a number of random points and then pick the one that is most distant of your choices
  • Poisson Algorithm: Place all points a between a min and max distance away from a previous point
  • Gridplace: drawing a grid on the space and then randomly placing one point in a each grid square
  • Physics: placing all points on the board randomly, then treating them as points of equal mass and running a repulsive simulation on them (imagine them all being positive charges)
  • Random: simple random
